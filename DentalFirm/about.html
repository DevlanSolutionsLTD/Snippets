About Us

Brief History.

Solo Dental Care Clinic is an established family clinic providing general and specialized high-quality dental care to infants, children, adolescents and adults in Nairobi –Kenya since 2009. We provide a wide range of procedures in modern dentistry.

The practice is duly registered and the dental practitioners hold up to date registration with the Kenya Medical Practitioners and Dentists Board. We put your needs first to offer personalized, efficient, and comprehensive care.

We provide a full range of services including Dental check-up, dental & oral health education, routine cleanings, sealants. We provide fillings, root canal treatments, orthodontics, teeth replacements, aesthetic (cosmetic) and much more for you and your child in a compassionate manner.

Our focus is prevention and dental care to enable you to take control of your dental health and ensure a lifetime of beautiful smiles!
